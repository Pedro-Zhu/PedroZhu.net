2021 Greater London Homicide Data Exploration (WIP)

This is my first personal project.
I got the idea for the project because I came across the Metropolitan Police Service Homicide dashboard data, which recorded all homicides that took place in Greater London from March 2003 until March 2022. When I saw it, I also remembered that the UK Census took place in 2021 and if I mix the 2 of them, I could have somewhat accurate data that shows how likely it is for you to encounter a murderer in Greater London.
I imported the Excel tables into SQL Server, then I did some queries to look around at what I had, and did some cleaning since the homicide data has recordings starting from 2003, and I only wanted the ones in 2021, after that, I connected the 2 tables through the borough name. To finish it, I imported it all into Tableau, made graphs and a map showing the number of murderers, and, in the end, put it all together into a dashboard.

Datasets:

  • MPS Homicide dashboard data
  • England and Whales Census 2021
